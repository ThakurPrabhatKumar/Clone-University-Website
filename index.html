Intermediate is the first step toward career-oriented education. Generally, students take primary and secondary education less seriously but when they get to the intermediate level (college), it is time to take things a lot seriously because this is where careers can be constructed on solid ground.

Under-graduate

An Undergraduate or undergrad is a student who is studying for a bachelor’s degree and is still in the process of acquiring his/her undergraduate degree. Once these students finish their studies at the undergraduate college and get their degrees, they are considered graduates.

Post-graduation

Post graduation course is a master degree which is done after graduation. Masters degree is done to do specialization in some fiel. Most of the post graduation courses are completed in the period of two years but there are some courses which take a period of three years for completion.
